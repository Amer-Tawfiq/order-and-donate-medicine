About Us

Your Charity Pharmacy is dedicated to reducing medicine waste and ensuring surplus medicines reach those in need. We believe in a world where everyone has access to essential medications.

Our Mission

To facilitate the donation of surplus medicines and connect them with individuals, hospitals, and charities that need them.

Our Vision

Creating a global impact by minimizing medicine waste and maximizing health benefits through responsible redistribution.

Our Team

We are a group of passionate individuals, dedicated to improving access to healthcare through innovative solutions.
